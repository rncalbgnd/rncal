ABOUT ME!

I come from a humble family, with my father working as a tricycle driver and
my mother caring for my sister with Down syndrome. Despite our challenges,
I am driven to excel in my studies as a way of showing gratitude. With the support
of my aunt, I've been able to pursue my education in Manila. I'm currently studying
Information Technology at Technological University of the Philippines Manila.
My goal is to provide my family with a house, and this motivates me to work hard and
complete my studies.
